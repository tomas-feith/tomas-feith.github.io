Senior Research Scientist• DeepL• Rotterdam, NL• Sep 2024 – Present

Research & LLM Development at DeepL

Taking large language models from research idea to production — training, evaluation, and rollout of models for text improvement and translation that reach millions of users.

DeepL Write, overhauled

I led an overhaul of DeepL Write — DeepL's writing-assistant models — across all supported languages. The new models win the majority of head-to-head comparisons against every dedicated competitor and reach parity with GPT-4-class LLMs on internal preference evaluations.

Launching new languages end-to-end

I brought Japanese, Chinese, and Korean Write models from scratch into production — owning SFT, evaluation, and rollout — unlocking three new key markets. This meant working closely with language specialists to define evaluation metrics that actually capture quality in each language.

Document translation: pipelines & evaluation

Beyond text improvement, I've worked on translating whole documents. I helped build a multi-stage pipeline for translating Office documents (starting with PowerPoint), chaining extraction, translation, and layout reconstruction so that heterogeneous, real-world slides survive translation intact.

To measure quality at scale, I built an evaluation framework for document translation that scores outputs across multiple quality dimensions — replacing a slow, qualitative, gut-feel process with a reliable, quantitative signal and cutting evaluation turnaround from days to hours.

How I work

  • Research that ships — every project above went to production, not just a paper or a demo.
  • Full model lifecycle — data curation and augmentation, SFT, distillation, evaluation, and deployment.
  • Rigorous evaluation — designing the metric is often the hard part; I care about measuring the right thing before optimising it.
  • Mentoring — I've onboarded a new team member end-to-end, getting them to meaningful contributions quickly.
