About

I'm an ML/AI research scientist working at the intersection of research and production. At DeepL I lead work on large-scale language-model training — SFT, RLHF/RLAIF, distillation, and evaluation — turning research ideas into models that reach millions of users.

My background spans Computational Science & Engineering (MSc, EPFL) and Engineering Physics (BEng, Instituto Superior Técnico). Along the way I've published first-author papers at EMNLP 2024 and SPIE 2023, filed 6 US patents on code-aware LLMs, and represented Portugal at the International Physics Olympiad.

I care about rigorous evaluation, multilingual systems, and squeezing state-of-the-art quality out of efficient, deployable models.
